Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive structures of flowering plants (angiosperms). They are designed to facilitate reproduction, often by attracting pollinators like bees, butterflies, birds, and bats. Flowers are admired for their beauty, fragrance, and symbolic meanings across cultures.

Roses pictures

The rose is one of the most famous and beloved flowers worldwide, known for its beauty, fragrance, and symbolism. Roses belong to the genus Rosa and the family Rosaceae. They have been cultivated for thousands of years and come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors.
